📖 Vocabulary #5

Personality Adjectives
12 words · Cambridge Dictionary Definitions · Visual Examples
🗣️ Speaking Unit · 2026
📌 About This Set

These 12 personality adjectives describe character traits and social qualities. Use them to talk about yourself and others in speaking and writing tasks.

1
AFFECTIONATE
/əˈfek.ʃən.ət/ · adjective
▼
📖 Definition

Showing feelings of liking or love for someone in a gentle and kind way.

💬 Example

"She is very affectionate with her younger siblings — always hugging them."

💛 warm 🤗 loving ❌ cold
2
SENSITIVE
/ˈsen.sɪ.tɪv/ · adjective
▼
📖 Definition

Able to understand the feelings of other people and aware of their needs; also easily affected emotionally.

💬 Example

"He is so sensitive that even small comments can hurt his feelings."

🧠 empathetic ❌ insensitive
3
SENSIBLE
/ˈsen.sɪ.bl̩/ · adjective
▼
📖 Definition

Showing good judgement and reason; practical and wise. (⚠️ Not the same as "sensitive"!)

💬 Example

"It was sensible of her to study in advance instead of waiting for the last day."

🧩 reasonable 💡 practical ❌ foolish
4
OPEN-MINDED
/ˌəʊ.pənˈmaɪn.dɪd/ · adjective
▼
📖 Definition

Willing to consider ideas and opinions that are different from your own.

💬 Example

"A good teacher is open-minded and always listens to different perspectives."

🌍 tolerant ❌ narrow-minded
5
BRIGHT
/braɪt/ · adjective
▼
📖 Definition

Clever and quick to learn; intelligent.

💬 Example

"She is a bright student who always understands new concepts quickly."

🧠 clever ✨ sharp ❌ dull
6
CHARMING
/ˈtʃɑː.mɪŋ/ · adjective
▼
📖 Definition

Very pleasing or attractive in a way that makes people like you.

💬 Example

"Everyone liked the new student — he was incredibly charming and funny."

😊 delightful ✨ captivating ❌ unpleasant
7
ENTHUSIASTIC
/ɪnˌθjuː.ziˈæs.tɪk/ · adjective
▼
📖 Definition

Feeling or showing a lot of excitement and interest about something.

💬 Example

"The team was enthusiastic about the project and worked extra hours to finish it."

🔥 passionate ⚡ eager ❌ apathetic
8
AMBITIOUS
/æmˈbɪʃ.əs/ · adjective
▼
📖 Definition

Having a strong desire to be successful, powerful, or rich.

💬 Example

"She is very ambitious and wants to become a doctor by the time she's 25."

🎯 driven 💪 determined ❌ unmotivated
9
SOCIABLE
/ˈsəʊ.ʃə.bl̩/ · adjective
▼
📖 Definition

Friendly and enjoying spending time with other people.

💬 Example

"He's very sociable and makes friends wherever he goes."

🤝 outgoing 👥 friendly ❌ withdrawn
10
SELF-CONFIDENT
/ˌself ˈkɒn.fɪ.dənt/ · adjective
▼
📖 Definition

Feeling sure about your own ability to do things and be successful.

💬 Example

"She walked into the interview self-confident and answered every question without hesitation."

💎 assured 🦁 bold ❌ insecure
11
RESPECTFUL
/rɪˈspekt.fəl/ · adjective
▼
📖 Definition

Showing polite feelings of admiration and care towards other people.

💬 Example

"The students were very respectful during the assembly, listening quietly."

🙏 polite 🎓 considerate ❌ rude
12
TEAM PLAYER
/ˈtiːm ˌpleɪ.ər/ · noun phrase
▼
📖 Definition

Someone who works well as part of a group and puts the group's goals before their own.

💬 Example

"We need a team player for this project — someone who collaborates well with others."

🤝 cooperative 🏆 collaborative ❌ self-centered
